1. If this is your first move play inside your area
    1.1. If you are an inside player you play in the area inside the four main corners 
    1.2. If you are an outside player you play in the area outside the four main corners

2. Each move you make after must be played within the area inside the perimeter of the last move you made and the last move the opponent made

3. If there are no free spaces in that area, expand the playable area by 1 cell on every side.

4. Repeat step 3 until an a valid move has been found
